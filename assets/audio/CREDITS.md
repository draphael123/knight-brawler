# Audio credits

All recorded audio in this folder is released under Creative Commons Zero (CC0).

- `hope-battle.ogg` — “Hope (Orchestral Battle Music)” by MintoDog, downloaded from OpenGameArt.org. Used as the heroic boss theme.
- `sword-swing-*.ogg`, `sword-hit-*.ogg`, and `sword-block.ogg` — selected and renamed sounds from “20 Sword Sound Effects (Attacks and Clashes)” by StarNinjas, downloaded from OpenGameArt.org.

The five regular level themes and supporting sound layers are generated in the game with Web Audio.

Sources:

- https://opengameart.org/content/hopeorchestral-battle-music
- https://opengameart.org/content/20-sword-sound-effects-attacks-and-clashes
- https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/
